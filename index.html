Project 1: 2D Digital Design (Southworth Planetarium Logo)

⭐🌙

  • Project Statement: This illustraion was designed as a potential logo for the University of Southern Maine's Southworth Planetarium.
    There is a husky (the mascot of USM) face in the middle of the circle surrounded by three stars, one on the top and one on each side of the dog.
    It uses the official university colors, gold and navy blue for the stars and background.

Project 3: Video Art (Hydra)

🐉🐍

  • Project Statement: Two videos were cut and spliced together to create one video.
    The final video is in black and white with no sound.
    In the beginning, a title card with the words, "The Living Cell" are shown followed by a sequence of a mircoscope zooming in to the titular character the Hydra.
    The rest of the film switching between shots of a hydra cell and of a cartoo dinosaur which is assumed to also be named Hydra.

  • Program Used: Adobe Premiere Pro

Project 4: Interactive Projection (Origami Cranes)

📄🏗

  • Project Statement: For this last project, coding and video editing were combined to create an interaction projection.
    The input AKA a sensor was coded so that when motion was detected a video would play of a piece of paper being folded into an origami crane.
    Ideally, the auidence member would fold along with the film (digital twinning).

  • Program Used: Adobe Premiere Pro, Arduino IDE, and Touch Designer

This folder contains the Arduino and Touch Designer codes.
